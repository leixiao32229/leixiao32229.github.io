Biography

Welcome to my site! I am an Assistant Professor at HKU Business School. I earned my Ph.D. in Operations Research from Columbia University under the guidance of Prof. Adam Elmachtoub. My research is centered on designing enjoyable and sustainable user engagement. Much of my recent work focuses on two major themes:

  1. Repeated and sequential user experiences in emerging digital platforms, particularly in the gaming and education industries.

  2. Socially responsible practices in revenue management and pricing, including fairness, anti-collusion, anti-addiction, and more.

My research has been acknowledged with several prestigious awards, such as

  • INFORMS George B. Dantzig Dissertation Award (Honorable Mention);
  • INFORMS Revenue Management & Pricing Section Prize;
  • INFORMS Behavioral OM Best Working Paper Award (Second Place);
  • INFORMS Service Science Best Student Paper Award (First & Third Place).

I also have more than 50,000 followers on Zhihu, a Chinese knowledge sharing platform.

I’m looking for self-motivated RAs/PhDs/Visiting PhDs/Postdocs with good mathematical or engineering foundations. Please review my papers and email your CV and transcripts.
