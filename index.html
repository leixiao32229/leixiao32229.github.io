Biography

Welcome to my site! I am an assistant professor at HKU Business School. I got my PhD in operations research from Columbia University, and I’m fortunate to be advised by Prof. Adam Elmachtoub. My research spans two major themes:

  1. Operations management in emerging digital markets, with a current focus on video games.
  2. Promoting responsible operations in profit-driven businesses.

In the summer of 2020, I worked as a data scientist intern at Activision Blizzard.
